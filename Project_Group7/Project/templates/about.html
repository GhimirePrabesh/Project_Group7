The dataset has 13 columns, and the detail is provided in the section “The Description of Dataset”.

Description of Dataset

Invoice id: Unique alphanumeric identifier for invoices, ensuring data integrity.

Branch: Categorical field for branch names where transactions occur, aiding in sales analysis.

City: Location of supercenters, categorized to understand regional sales trends.

Customer type: Categorizes customers as "Normal" or "Member," guiding marketing strategies.

Gender: Indicates customer gender as "Male" or "Female," valuable for demographic analysis.

Product line: Categorizes items for sales analysis and inventory management.

Unit price: Price of each product in dollars, crucial for revenue calculation.

Quantity: Number of products purchased per transaction, essential for inventory management.

Tax: Calculates 5% tax fee for purchases, ensuring financial compliance.

Total: Total transaction price including tax, vital for revenue analysis.

Time: Purchase time, aiding in understanding peak sales hours.

Payment: Mode of payment used, guiding payment processing and customer preferences.

COGS: Cost of goods sold, crucial for calculating profit margins.
